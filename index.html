Built on Community Trust

Our journey began as a humble shop specializing in fresh lemons, juice, and water cans, carrying forward the legacy of our family’s Sri Subramaniya Ricemill (Est. 1992).


Today, we have grown into a complete neighborhood store offering a comprehensive range of products and services. Built on a decade of personal service, what started with a simple mission has blossomed into a one-stop destination for all your daily needs.
